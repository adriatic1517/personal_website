Hello.

This is one of the few digital dictionaries of Pashto.

It is a digital version of a dictionary written about 200 years ago.

It is backed by a custom and fast search algorithm based on Tf-idf.

I created it with the help of these kind people.

This is the beginning of what I hope to be a broader project of making Pashto a language of the internet.

I have plans for digitizing Pashto literature.

I present this in its current form, in the hopes that perhaps 200 years in the future, someone will build upon it.

This work builds on the University of Chicago's Digital Dictionaries of South Asia project

If you would like to know more about this project, read my blog post (coming soon) on it.
