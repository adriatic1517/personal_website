This is a Pashto to English dictionary written about 200 years ago by Henry George Raverty, a British colonial linguist. It was first digitized by the University of Chicago's Digital Dictionaries of South Asia project.

This is the beginning of what I hope to be a broader project of digitizing Pashto language and literature.

Some features that this project adds on are a very fast, scalable search algorithm, responsive interface and accessibility.
