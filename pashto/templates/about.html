This is one of the few dictionaries of Pashto in digital form.

I created it with the help of these kind people.

This is the beginning of what I hope to be a broader project of digitizing literature in the Persio-Arabic script.

I hope that sometime in the future, Pashto literature is accessible and Pashto can be learnt online

I present this in its current form, in the hopes that perhaps it might be useful to someone.

If you would like to know more about this project or are interested in the technical aspects, read my blog post on it.
